About FEWD

Front-end web development involves designing and implementing the user interface and interactions of a website or web application. It primarily focuses on HTML for structure, CSS for styling, and JavaScript for behavior. Front-end developers work to create a visually appealing and responsive experience, ensuring a seamless interaction between users and the web application. They use frameworks like React, Angular, or Vue.js to build dynamic and interactive user interfaces. Front-end development is crucial for delivering a positive user experience and is an integral part of the overall web development process.

About Personal Finance Tracker

Personal Finance Tracker empowers students to effortlessly monitor their savings and expenses, providing a convenient platform for gaining insights into their monthly financial activities. This tool serves as a valuable resource for obtaining a comprehensive overview of your financial status. Users can input their expenses and savings, and the platform visualizes this data through interactive graphs, offering a clear and intuitive representation of their financial landscape. With the Personal Finance Tracker, managing and understanding your finances becomes an accessible and insightful experience.
